# Brand Brief

## Positioning
- **Business:** AB Electricians — licensed Sydney electricians serving North Shore and Greater Sydney
- **Owner:** Adam Bushell
- **Ideal Customer:** Sydney homeowners and businesses who need a reliable, licensed electrician — prioritise on-time service, transparent pricing, and quality guaranteed work. Often urgent (emergency callouts), always looking for someone they can trust.
- **Primary CTA:** "Book Now" or "Call Now" — urgent conversion
- **Secondary CTA:** "Get a Free Quote"
- **Differentiator:** Highest-reviewed electrician in Sydney. Lifetime Workmanship Warranty. Local North Shore team. Show up when they say, upfront written pricing, leave the site as clean as they found it.
- **Positioning Statement:** AB Electricians is the highest-reviewed licensed electrical contractor on Sydney's North Shore — a local team that shows up on time, gives you a written upfront price before any work starts, and backs every job with a Lifetime Workmanship Warranty. From residential switchboards to Level 2 connections, they treat your home or business like their own.

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Personality:** Local, direct, trustworthy
- **Tone:** Confident and experienced — like a trusted tradie having a straight conversation. Not salesy. Not corporate. Not casual-slangy. Knowledgeable and warm.
- **We are:** On-time, transparent, licensed, local, family-friendly, quality-first
- **We are NOT:** Corporate, pushy, vague, generic, nationwide-chain-feeling

## Visual Identity

### Color Constraints
- **Anchor colors:** Navy #011835, teal-blue #007cba, electric blue #00a3ff, green accent #00d082, orange #fb8e28
- **Avoid:** Purple tones, pink

### Font Constraints
- **Brand fonts (existing):** Archivo (headings), Inter (body) — used in Direction 1. Directions 2 and 3 may use alternative pairings.

### Visual Style
- **Keywords:** Professional, local, trustworthy, energetic, clean
- **Inspiration patterns:** Trade business site with strong credentials, fleet photography, Sydney backdrop imagery
- **Do:** Use real team/van/fleet photography, display certifications prominently, lead with the highest-reviewed claim, use urgency cues for emergency service
- **Don't:** Stock imagery, generic iconography, corporate wordsmithing, vague "quality service" language
