## Voice Rules (critical — enforce in all copy)

- **Australian English** always (organise, colour, licence as noun, metres)
- **No em dashes** — ever. Use commas or new sentences.
- **Sentence case** for headlines (not Title Case)
- **"We/us"** inside copy, **"Plumber [Suburb]"** in titles and structured data
- **"You" and "your home"** for customer reference — never "client" or "customer"
- Sentences: 12–18 words average; short and punchy preferred
- Numerals for 10+, prices, times, stats; spell out 1–9 in body copy
- No superlatives: best, leading, top-rated, world-class, premier are banned
- No corporate filler: solutions, seamless, holistic, leverage, end-to-end are banned
- Contractions encouraged: we're, don't, you'll, it's

## Suburb Localisation Rules

- Name the suburb in the opening sentence of every page
- Suburb name max 3–4 times per 300 words
- Reference real local context (older homes, neighbouring suburbs, landmarks)
- Do not clone suburb paragraphs with only the name swapped
