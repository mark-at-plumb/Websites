# Your New Site Plan

## Brand Brief
Aptec Plumbing is a licensed plumber servicing Happy Valley & southern Adelaide, 24/7. Target customer: local homeowners/small businesses needing fast, transparent, no-fuss plumbing — from urgent blocked drains and burst pipes to scheduled hot water/gas work. Primary CTA: call (08) 8000 5050 or "Book Online." Differentiators: $0 call-out fee, same-day/24-7 emergency response, upfront pricing, licensed (Lic. #333997, ABN 52 668 135 886), family-run feel.

## Visual Direction
- Color/font constraints: None mandated, but must stay within a blue plumbing-trade trust palette to match the reference site's look and feel.
- Vibe: Trustworthy, fast, no-fuss, local, clean.

## Concept Directions
All 3 directions reuse the **identical homepage layout and section order** above — per your instruction to keep the same look and feel. They differ only in palette shade, type pairing, and card/shape styling.

### Direction 1: Trade Blue
**Palette:** Trade Blue #0B5FFF · Navy #0B2847 · Green accent #11B76B on White #FFFFFF
**Fonts:** Manrope (headings) + Inter (body)
Classic plumbing-trade blue on white, closest to the reference site. Rounded cards, soft shadows, bold confident headlines, green "$0 Call Out" badge as accent.
**Why it fits:** Safest, most recognizable match to the original site's look.

### Direction 2: Site Authority
**Palette:** Navy #0B2847 · Steel #1B3C5C · Safety Orange #FF7A1A on Off-white #FBFBFA
**Fonts:** Sora (headings) + IBM Plex Sans (body)
Deeper navy/steel tones, warm orange CTA accent, flat elevation, sharper edges, uppercase section labels — more rugged/industrial trade authority feel.
**Why it fits:** Leans into licensed-tradesman trust with a navy + orange combo, same exact structure.

### Direction 3: Bright Service
**Palette:** Sky Blue #167EE6 · Navy #0B2847 · Lime Green #00D084 on White #FFFFFF
**Fonts:** Plus Jakarta Sans (headings) + DM Sans (body)
Lighter, friendlier palette pulled from the existing promo badge colors in your images. Pill buttons, dramatic soft shadows, more whitespace — modern "book online" feel.
**Why it fits:** Pulls accents straight from your supplied assets for a fresher, more digital-native feel while staying unmistakably the same brand.
