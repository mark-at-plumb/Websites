# Brand Brief

## Positioning
- **Business:** Aptec Plumbing — a family-owned, 24/7 emergency plumbing company serving Greater Sydney.
- **Ideal Customer:** Sydney homeowners (and small property managers) facing an urgent plumbing issue — blocked drains, leaking taps, broken hot water systems — who want a fast, fairly-priced, no-surprises tradesperson.
- **Primary CTA:** Call now (0420-507-300) / Book Online for same-day service.
- **Differentiator:** $0 call-out fee, upfront pricing, 15+ years family-owned, 5.0 stars from 236 reviews, same-day & 24/7 emergency response.
- **Positioning Statement:** Aptec Plumbing is the trusted, transparent local plumber for Greater Sydney — family-run for 15+ years, available 24/7, with upfront pricing and zero call-out fees. "Clear. Fair. Simple."

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Personality:** Trustworthy, no-nonsense, approachable
- **Tone:** Direct, reassuring, locally-rooted
- **We are:** Reliable, fast, transparent, family-owned, experienced
- **We are NOT:** Corporate/faceless, pushy/upsell-heavy, vague on pricing

## Visual Identity

### Color Constraints (locked — redesign, keep branding)
Extracted live from aptecplumbing.com.au CSS custom properties:
- **Primary blue:** `#156ECE`
- **Accent orange (CTA):** `#FF5700`
- **Dark navy (text/footer):** `#1b3c5c`
- **Body gray:** `#4a4a4a`
- **Off-white:** `#dbdbdb`
- **White:** `#ffffff`
- **Avoid:** Introducing unrelated brand colors (greens, purples) outside this palette.

### Font Constraints (locked)
- Original site uses "Cal Sans" (headings) + "Google Sans" (body) — both proprietary/unavailable via Google Fonts.
- **Substitute, locked across all directions:** Plus Jakarta Sans (headings) + Inter (body).

### Visual Style
- **Keywords:** Trustworthy, energetic, local, clean, professional-trade
- **Inspiration patterns:** Split hero with team photo, coupon/promo cards, icon-led service grid, alternating text/image feature sections, numbered process steps, suburb-based service area listing, team photo strip.
- **Do:** Strong orange CTA buttons, blue trust accents, real trade photography, scroll/hover animations for energy.
- **Don't:** Generic stock-photo look, flat corporate blue without warmth, cluttered nav.
